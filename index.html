Privacy Policy for LEXTIDE

Last Updated: March 9, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how LEXTIDE collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Chrome Extension: LEXTIDE – Terms, privacy, licenses policies check.

Information We Collect

LEXTIDE collects only the minimum data necessary to provide its services:

  • User Activity: Interactions with our extension (e.g., clicks, usage patterns).
  • Website Content: The content from the pages you visit, specifically policy documents (terms of service, privacy policies, and licenses).

We do NOT collect or store:

  • Personally identifiable information (name, email, etc.)
  • Health, financial, or authentication information
  • Personal communications or location data
  • Browsing history beyond what's necessary for policy analysis

How We Use Information

Your data is exclusively used to:

  • Analyze and summarize policy documents on the websites you visit.
  • Highlight potential red flags or concerning terms.
  • Provide intuitive ratings (A-F) about policy fairness and safety.

Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your information to third parties, except for sending content to OpenAI’s API solely for generating summaries. By using LEXTIDE, you agree to OpenAI’s Terms of Service.

Data Storage & Security

We temporarily store the analyzed results within the browser storage on your device. We take appropriate technical measures to safeguard this data.

Your Rights

You have complete control over the data stored in your browser and may delete it anytime by uninstalling the extension or clearing your browser data.

Disclaimer

Summaries provided by LEXTIDE are AI-generated and should not be considered legal advice. LEXTIDE is not responsible for inaccuracies or incomplete information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be reflected here with an updated revision date.
